About Jeevan Gopal

My journey in the entertainment industry commenced at a young age, as I stepped into the limelight as a child artist in both Malayalam films and television serials. Through dedication and a passion for storytelling, I quickly garnered attention for my talent and commitment to the craft.
One of my most recognizable roles came to life in the popular Malayalam film "Alavudinte Albutha Vilakku," where I portrayed the character of Jeem-boom-ba, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of viewers. Additionally, I had the privilege of essaying the protagonist, Mahesh, in the acclaimed 2018 soap opera serial "Makkal," which aired on Mazhavil Manorama.

Movies
